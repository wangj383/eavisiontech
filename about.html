About EAVision

Real solutions for a better tomorrow

We strive to bring a positive impact to the environment while increasing efficiency in the fields. Our advanced autonomous systems are designed with the intention to minimize waste while making the jobs at hand easier and safer for the people involved.

Agriculture is just the beginning

Our current application focuses on the agriculture industry, but we are always looking to the future. The technologies we are developing and researching today can be applied to other industries as well such as track inspection, search and rescue, power grid inspection, logistics, and mapping.

We are passionate about making an impact

We are a close-knit team based in Silicon Valley with a combined 100+ years of industry experience. Our foundation as a team was built on our passion to develop technologies that make a positive impact on people and the environment. Starting from drones for agriculture, we are redefining how UAVs work in an industrial setting.

Meet the team

Dr. James Dong

CEO

PhD in Electrical Engineering at CREOL Optical Center of University of Central Florida, MS at Jilin University. Expert in the fields of optics and machine vision.
Over the past twenty years, Dr. Dong has worked at many Silicon Valley companies, including LuxNet Corp., Agility Comm, and JDSU companies. He has founded IITECH, co-founded New Vision Science and Technology Co., and was the VP of LuxNet Corp.

Dr. Han-Shue Tan

CTO

MS and PhD in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley. Expert in dynamics, guidance, control, and safety systems.
Dr. Tan was a staff systems design architect at Tesla Motors responsible for the technical coordination between Active Safety and Autopilot Control Groups. He has published over 100 papers along with 8 US patents and 5 US patent applications.

Dr. Jill Huang

Chief Scientist

PhD in Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley and MS at Tsinghua University. Expert in control system design and signal processing.
Dr. Huang developed the first automated steering bus in revenue service in the US. She was a senior system analyst at Intuitive Surgical, a senior research engineer at General Motors, and the chief scientist at ContainerTrac Inc.

Partnership

We work closely with our partners in China to pursue our vision of creating advanced autonomous systems.
We have a manufacture and hardware design center in Suzhou, a software development center in Wuhan, and an agriculture partner in Henan and Xinjiang.
