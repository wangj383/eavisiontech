Benefits

ACCESSIBLE

Our drones have advanced decision–making, machine learning, planning abilities, and safety management, making it accessible to use without extensive knowledge of drones.

Its ability to communicate with other drones and back–end servers also allow for efficient operations.

PRECISE

With distance sensing, sensor fusion, and real time terrain scanning, our drones can automatically maintain the right distance and path for the most precise spray.

This allows for a more efficient use of fertilizer and pesticide, resulting in less resources wasted and less harmful pesticides introduced to the environment.

SMART

Our drones are highly adaptable and able to learn new and changing terrain.

Advanced technologies in obstacle avoidance (less than one foot of GPS position error) also enable users to spend less time worrying about entering route maps and more time doing what matters to them.

OPERATION

Portable. Quick setup. Easy to operate.
